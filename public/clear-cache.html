This page will help you clear all cached data and unregister the service worker to fix the "Server Action not found" error.

What gets cleared:

  1. All service worker caches (v1, v2, v3)
  2. Service worker registration
  3. Local storage
  4. Session storage
  5. IndexedDB

  2. Or visit chrome://serviceworker-internals/ and unregister
